Intercomparison of aircraft and ground based measurements near major pollution sources over Taiwan (Ongoing)

In this project, I analyzed aircraft-based observation data and compared them with surface measurements and model results to investigated near surface chemical reactions of pollutants.

#aircraft-based observation #pollution analysis #intercomparison

Tracer distribution simulation in western Taiwan using high resolution Taiwan Vector Vorticity Model (Ongoing)

In this project, I analyzed simulation results and evaluated the impact on target regions, which usually have terrible air pollutions. In the next stage, we aim to applied zero and first-order chemical reactions into the model to inspect lifetime effect on the regions so as to understand the sources of the pollutant in those highly-polluted regions.

#model simulation #pollution analysis #lifetime effect

Quantification of glucose via in situ growth of Cuprous oxide/Silver nanoparticles

A single-step method combining material preparation and the actual glucose measurements via the in situ growth of Cu2O/Ag nanoparticles was reported. Mechanistically, I use glucose itself to reduce Cu2+ and Ag+ ions that are stabilized with 3-mercaptopropionic acid and bovine serum albumin under alkaline conditions to form Cu2O/Ag nanoparticles and produce a color change in solution due to plasmonic resonance at 400 nm, which can be related back to the concentration of glucose.

#Cu2O/Ag nanoparticles #in situ glucose quantification #colorimetric analysis

Removing mercury ions from polluted solution through poly-dopamine-modified Iron Oxide nanoparticles

In this study, I improved the production efficiency of Fe3O4 nanoparticles by using a novel microwave synthesizer. Also, by modifying a poly-dopamine layer on the surface of nanoparticles to increase the surface area of nanoparticles, I succeeded in enlarging the Hg2+ ions adsorption capacity and releasing efficiency under hydrochloric acid of poly-dopamine-modified Fe3O4 nanoparticles and maintaining the high magnetic property of the material. The magnetic property of poly-dopamine-modified Fe3O4 nanoparticles makes it eco-friendly to reuse the material.

#Fe3O4 nanoparticles #Hg2+ removal #eco-friendly nanomaterial
